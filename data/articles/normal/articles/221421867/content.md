<p>Where an announcements channel might not get the job done, Discord has a <strong>pin messages</strong> function that'll allow you to have quick access to <strong>important messages</strong> from way far back or as recently as you like.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360058839912/Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_11.18.07_AM.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_11.18.07_AM.png" width="566" height="435"> </p>
<h1>Adding New Pins </h1>
<p>Ready your thumbtacks and start pinning away!</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><span class="wysiwyg-underline"><strong><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360058995571/pinning_message_demo.gif" alt="pinning_message_demo.gif" width="528" height="407"></strong></span></p>
<p>To pin a message, just <strong>click on the "three dots"/More icon</strong> at the far right of the message:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360059015011/Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_3.07.35_PM.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_3.07.35_PM.png" width="482" height="459"></p>
<p>If your role has the <strong>Manage Messages </strong>permission (or you're the server owner), you'll see the "<strong>Pin Message</strong>" feature in the drop-down menu.</p>
<p>Right-clicking on the message will also work too!</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360058857512/Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_3.04.23_PM.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_3.04.23_PM.png" width="385" height="246"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Once you confirm that you want to pin the message, you will save that message to the pin window!</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360059015251/Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_10.43.01_AM.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_10.43.01_AM.png" width="390" height="508"></p>
<p><em>As the prompt says, make sure you double check for posterity and greatness. These aren't just any common messages, you know.</em></p>
<h3><em>Note: You can only have 50 pins per channel/DM so make sure to pin your messages wisely!</em></h3>
<h1>Viewing All Pinned Messages</h1>
<p><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Collapsable Corkboard, Endless Time Warp:</span></strong></p>
<p>Any pinned message will appear in the pin window, accessed by clicking the pin icon in the top-right corner:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360059015791/Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_10.43.43_AM.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_10.43.43_AM.png" width="538" height="211"></p>
<h1>Jumping to Pinned Message</h1>
<p>Another important point is that no matter how long ago the pinned message is, you can click the "<strong>jump</strong>" link next to the pinned message in the pin window to jump all the way back to when that message was originally written:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360058840932/jump_to_pin_demo.gif" alt="jump_to_pin_demo.gif" width="557" height="429"></p>
<h1>Removing Pins</h1>
<p>Removing pins when you no longer need them or need to delete old pins for more is also easy peasy! </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360058857052/deleting_pin_demo.gif" alt="deleting_pin_demo.gif" width="541" height="417"></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">When you're ready to unpin a message, just click the "<strong>X</strong>" icon next to that "<strong>Jump</strong>" button we saw earlier:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360058858752/Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_3.18.06_PM.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_3.18.06_PM.png" width="414" height="581"></p>
<p>And confirm:</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><img src="https://support.discord.com/hc/article_attachments/360059015951/Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_3.20.22_PM.png" alt="Screen_Shot_2020-06-05_at_3.20.22_PM.png" width="368" height="531"></p>
<h3>Last Protip: <br>By holding shift and clicking the "X" unpin icon in the pin window, you can bypass the above "kthx" unpinning message to unlock true rapid-fire unpinning madness.</h3>
